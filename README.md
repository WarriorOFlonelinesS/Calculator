# Operations 
## Summ
For summing choce the first number and click on the button with sign `+`. After choce the second number. In the end click on `=` .
## Substraction 
For summing choce the first number and click on the button with sign `-`. After choce the second number. In the end click on `=` .
## Multiplication
For summing choce the first number and click on the button with sign `*`. After choce the second number. In the end click on `=` .
## Division
